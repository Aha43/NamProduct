For power users & purists

Want it all on your own machine?

Most people never need to leave the browser. But if you want more room to work — or you would rather your tasks never touch a server — there is a full desktop app. NamDesktop is its own thing, for two kinds of people:

  • Power users who live in Nam all day and want a roomier workbench — and who pair the desktop with the browser app so they can capture on their phone too.
  • Old-school and privacy-minded folks who keep everything local: a single file on your own machine, with no cloud at all — or a simple Git sync you control.
